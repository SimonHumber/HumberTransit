Who Are We?

At Humber Tranist, we are a young team of 3 software developers looking to help our local community. We are Sagi, Simon and George and we intent to continue to work on Humber Transit for as long as it sevrves the community. In the future we also hope to reach out to regions outside of the GTA and provide more services with the upcoming LRT line.

Why Did We Start Humber Transit?

At Humber Transit, we belived that there needed to be a better way to get to and from the college campus. with so many different bus networks running to the terminal, it can become tedious to keep checking different websites when it comes to route scheduling. With over 83,000 combined full-time and part-time students studying at Humber, there needs to be a more convenient option.
